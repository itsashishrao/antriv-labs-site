TLMs vs LLMs

TLMs (Task-Specific)

  • Laser-focused models ≫ higher accuracy on domain tasks
  • Up to 10–50× cheaper to run in production
  • Predictable outputs; easier to validate & govern
  • Private deployment — bring the model to your data

LLMs (General-Purpose)

  • Broad reasoning, but inconsistent for narrow tasks
  • Higher latency & compute costs at scale
  • Output drift; harder to constrain precisely
  • Often requires sending data to third parties
